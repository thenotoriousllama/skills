## 3. Logo Usage

### Logo Variants

{{LOGO_VARIANTS}}

> Example:
> - Primary: Full logo with wordmark (use on white/light backgrounds)
> - Secondary: Icon only (use for social avatars, favicons)
> - Reversed: White version (use on dark backgrounds)

### Clear Space
- Minimum clear space around logo: {{LOGO_CLEAR_SPACE}}
- Never place logo on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast

### Logo Misuse
- Never stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
- Never change logo colors outside approved palette
- Never add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Never place on clashing or low-contrast backgrounds

## 5. Photography and Image Style

### Style Direction

{{PHOTO_STYLE}}

> Example:
> - Editorial quality, never stock photography aesthetic
> - Natural lighting preferred, studio lighting when controlled
> - Warm color temperature, subtle film grain
> - Subjects should look natural and intelligent, never overly posed

### Subject Direction
- {{PHOTO_SUBJECT_RULE_1}}
- {{PHOTO_SUBJECT_RULE_2}}
- Lead with intelligence and intention, not appearance
- Diverse representation in ethnicity, body type, and age

### Post-Production
- {{POST_PRODUCTION_STYLE}}
- Subtle film grain for warmth
- No heavy filters or dramatic color shifts
- Skin retouching should be minimal and natural

### Grid System

{{GRID_SYSTEM}}

### White Space Rules

- Minimum margins: {{MIN_MARGINS}}
